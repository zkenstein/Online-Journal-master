Why journal?

Good for You

Less Stress Expressive writing is an extremely cathartic process, helping you to relax and release stress.

Be Healthier Numerous studies have shown that keeping a journal can improve your immune function.

Get Smarter Keeping a journal or diary can not only boost your "working memory" but your grades too.

Lose Weight Keeping a food diary has been suggested to double weight-loss when combined with a diet.

Types of Journals

"Keep a diary, and someday it'll keep you" - Mae West
Daily Diary Keeping a daily diary with eJournal couldn't be easier. Write at any time from anywhere.

Expressive Journal Since eJournal is totally private, you can write whatever you want and truly express your feelings!

Travel Journal Going away? Record your experience with eJournal! Insert photos, send entries to friends, add tags, and more!

Why eJournal?

Never Lost or Stolen While a computer, or a paper-bound diary can be lost or stolen, your entries are always safe with eJournal.

Access Anywhere Ever forget your diary at home? Wherever you have internet, you have eJournal!

Privacy First Not only are your entries private, but you can password protect them individually for even more privacy.

Easy to Use With a simple design and paper-like interface, eJournal is extremely intuitive and easy to use.
